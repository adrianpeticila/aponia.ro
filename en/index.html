What is APONIA.ro?

APONIA.ro is a scheduling and practice management platform for independent psychotherapists. It replaces manual diary management and scattered WhatsApp messages with a clear system for appointments, confirmations, automatic notifications and session notes. The therapist pays for APONIA.ro; patients book for free without installing anything.

We spoke directly with 14 independent psychotherapists in Romania. The problem is not a lack of patients. It is time wasted on logistics. According to the European Federation of Psychologists' Associations (2023), psychologists in private practice spend 6-8 hours per week on admin. APONIA.ro reduces this to under 2 hours. Who built APONIA.ro

By APONIA.ro · Updated June 2026 · 14 psychotherapists consulted directly

I am not a therapist.
But I grew up around them.

I am surrounded by psychotherapists. I watched them closely, year after year, losing entire weekends to things that should have taken 15 minutes with a decent system. I watched them without free evenings, without free holidays, without the boundary they teach to others.

I did not see disorganized therapists. I saw serious professionals compensating daily, with their own time and energy, for the lack of a proper tool. People who knew exactly how to support someone else's boundary, and who had no system to support their own.

APONIA.ro is what I wished I could have given them sooner. Not a magic solution. A simple system that does what a well-organized practice should do, so they can do what they know how to do.

Who APONIA.ro is for

APONIA.ro is for you
if you recognize yourself in this:

You are a therapist with your own practice

Solo or with a few colleagues. You manage your own diary, no receptionist. Every hour of admin is an hour that is not therapy. Therapists with their own practice lose an average of 6-8 hours per week on scheduling and confirmations. With APONIA.ro, under 2 hours.

You have more than 8 active patients

Up to 8, a paper diary works. From 10 upward, the old system creates constant small crises and you manage them one by one.

You want to grow without growing the chaos

Each new patient should bring revenue, not 20-30 extra minutes of admin. At 10 active patients, that is 3-5 hours per week on messages, confirmations and rescheduling.

You want a real boundary, not just in theory

You know therapeutic boundary theory. But without a system, everything arrives directly to you: Saturday morning messages, 11pm confirmations, personal WhatsApp as a reception desk. APONIA.ro moves scheduling, confirmations and automatic notifications (24h and 1h before) to one place, so the boundary exists in practice, not just in theory.

APONIA.ro is not for you if you need integrated invoicing, video conferencing or a system for a clinic with a reception desk. APONIA.ro does one thing: organizes your practice so you can do therapy without consuming yourself.

Real questions

You are thinking about this.
We answer directly.

"My patients won't use a new app."

Your patients install nothing and create no account. They receive a link, open it in the browser, pick a slot. Under 90 seconds. In the beta with 14 psychotherapists, not a single patient refused to book online. It is simpler than sending you a WhatsApp.

"My patients' data is extremely sensitive."

Server in Frankfurt, Germany. AES-256 encryption in transit and at rest. Exclusive access to you — zero access by the APONIA.ro team to your patients' data. Native GDPR compliance, without reading any legal document. Your patients' data is not sold, not used for ads, not visible to other therapists.

"How long does setup take?"

15 minutes for a complete profile: availability, rate, booking link. First patient can book the same day. No tutorial, no step-by-step onboarding, no call with a consultant. If you have 15 free minutes between sessions today, you can be live today.

"What if it doesn't work for me?"

Cancel anytime from your account, on your own, without sending any email. No minimum period, no cancellation fee, no phone call with anyone. One click. If APONIA.ro doesn't give you back at least 3 hours/week of admin, it isn't worth the 73 RON.

"I already use Google Calendar. Why switch?"

Google Calendar does not send automatic notifications to your patients. It does not block occupied slots for other patients. It does not store session notes securely. It does not separate patient communication from personal WhatsApp. Therapists who used it before APONIA.ro reported 6-8 hours per week on admin, at least half on manual confirmations. It does one thing: keeps a calendar. APONIA.ro does everything else.

"Is APONIA.ro for psychologists or psychotherapists?"

Both. APONIA.ro is a scheduling and practice management platform for any specialist with individually scheduled sessions: psychologist, psychotherapist, counsellor, clinical coach. Beta therapists average 10-15 active clients. The only condition: one specialist per session. It does not work for groups or drop-in sessions.

"How do I know my data won't reach other platforms or marketplaces?"

APONIA.ro is not a marketplace and does not live off data: revenue comes from your 73 RON monthly subscription, not from your patients. Data is stored AES-256 encrypted on servers in Frankfurt, Germany, under GDPR. Your patients are not visible to other therapists, not recommended, not receiving ads. No data is shared with third parties. The relationship with the patient remains yours; APONIA.ro is only the scheduling infrastructure.
