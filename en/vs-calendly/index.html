A booking link is not a practice management system

Calendly is built for sales calls and interviews. The model is simple: someone sees a link, picks a slot, receives a calendar invite. Elegant, fast, works well for that.

Therapy does not work the same way. Your patient is not a prospect checking off a slot. They are a person you work with for weeks or months. You need to know what was discussed last time, send a discreet reminder the day before, note what's important after each session. Calendly does none of these things — and it never will, because they are not for its target audience.

APONIA.ro is built exclusively for therapists. Every product decision — how a patient profile looks, how notifications flow, how data is stored — is made with the context of therapy in mind.

Frequently asked questions

Calendly is cheaper. Why pay for APONIA.ro?

Calendly free doesn't include personalized notifications, has no session notes, and has no native language support for therapists. When you add a paid Calendly plan and workarounds for what's missing (Google Docs for notes, manual SMS for confirmations), the total cost grows. APONIA.ro does everything in one place.

Can I import data from Calendly into APONIA.ro?

Yes. APONIA.ro supports import from standard CSV files. If you have patient data in Calendly or any other system, the transition is straightforward.

Is my data stored in the EU?

Yes. APONIA.ro stores data on servers in the EU (Frankfurt, Germany), compliant with GDPR. Calendly stores on AWS in the USA with transfer mechanisms in place, but data does not remain in European jurisdiction.

Does it work for online therapy (video)?

Yes. APONIA.ro works whether sessions are in-person or online. You can add your video call link directly to the invitation sent to the patient.
