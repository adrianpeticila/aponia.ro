Common questions

Does it work for online therapy (video sessions)?

Yes. You add your video link (Zoom, Google Meet, or any other service) to your profile once, and it is included automatically in every booking confirmation. The patient gets the link without you having to send it each time.

Do I need a special license or accreditation to use APONIA.ro?

No. APONIA.ro is available to any therapist or psychologist with a private practice, regardless of certification body or license type. There is no verification step tied to professional credentials.

How does the session notes workflow look in practice?

After each session, you open the patient's profile in APONIA.ro and add your notes. They are timestamped, linked to that specific session, and visible only to you. The next time you see that patient, their history is one click away.

How is patient data secured?

Data is stored on servers in Frankfurt, Germany — within EU jurisdiction — with AES-256 encryption. APONIA.ro is designed to be compliant with GDPR Article 9, which covers sensitive health data at the highest protection level.
